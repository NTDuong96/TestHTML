Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. Here is a recipe for decliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1lb Butter
  • 2lbs Cream

Method

  1. Melt butter with a frying pan
  2. Mix eggs and cream (do it gently)
  3. Once butter had been melted add cream and eggs
  4. Fold the eggs, scroll or maybe make it in the way you want
  5. Take your spoon and have a nice meal!
